How It Works

For Real Food teaches you to grow real food, one step at a time, in the comfort of your own home. We'll deliver a series of 5 fun growing challenges that will incrementally teach you the fundamentals of growing your own food. By the end of the series, you will have the knowledge and confidence to continue growing food in your daily life.

What You Get

The For Real Food Intro Kit has everything you need to get started growing your own real food. Just add water and a sunny window! This kit includes:

  • Seeds for 4 different types of plants.
  • All the soil your plants could need.
  • Curated containers for each level.
  • Step by Step instructions to help you grow your green thumb.
  • An "Oops Kit", for those unexpected troubles.
  • Access to our ever-expanding video library and FAQs.
  • And a few surprises along the way!
