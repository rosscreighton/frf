How It Works

For Real Food teaches you to grow real food, one step at a time, in the comfort of your own home. We'll deliver a series of fun growing challenges that will incrementally teach you the fundamentals of growing your own food. By the end of the series, you will have the knowledge and confidence to continue growing plants in your daily life.

What You Get

Each growing challenge contains a growing tool kit with all the essential ingredients to get started. This tool kit is delivered to your door - just add water and a sunny window. Each kit comes with step by step instructions that take you through the learning process. We'll track your progress, unlocks new challenges, and send you tips and tricks as you progress.

Why Grow Real Food

Over half the world's population now lives in urban areas.

In 2008, for the first time in human history, the number of people living in urban areas exceeded the number living in rural areas. As cities continue to expand and urban populations grow, more food will need to come from rural areas. This will accelerate the physical and ideological disconnect between the act of consuming food and the practice of producing it.

In light of this disconnect, we see an opportunity.

We at For Real Food aim to empower urban-dwellers with limited gardening experience to reconnect to the natural environment and their food system by learning to cultivate plants through a series of fun growing challenges.
