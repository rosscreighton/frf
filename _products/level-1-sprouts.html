Overview

The first step to growing is sprouting the seed!

Many people think that soil is required for plants to grow, but sprouting a seed requires only water and the right environment. Sprouts eventually grow into full-sized plants, but they can also be harvested early for a tasty and nutrient-filled snack!

For this initial challenge, we will provide you with a kit that includes all the items and instructions you need to successfully grow sprouts. Once you complete the challenge, you will have a tasty group of baby green sprouts that you can enjoy, and the confidence to complete this essential first step in growing!
